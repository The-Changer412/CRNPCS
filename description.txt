Constant Randoms NPCs Spawning is a mod where Every 5 minutes (can be change in the config), a random npc will spawn in.
you can type "/timer" into the game's chat to see how much time is left before random NPCs will spawn in.

Source Code: https://github.com/The-Changer412/CRNPCS
